Notes:

Press 'p' key to pause

Vehicles are small triangles and predators are the larger.

Debug Menu Key:
The green circle, around each vehicle, is food perception radius.
The red circle is poison perception.
The grey circle is vehicle speed.
The green line is food weight and red line is poison weight.

If extinction mode is checked vehicles and predators will spawn at random.

If omnivore mode is checked predators will eat food and vehicles.

All vehicles with above 75% health have twice the chance of cloning themselves.
